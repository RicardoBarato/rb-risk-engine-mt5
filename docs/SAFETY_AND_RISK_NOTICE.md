# Safety and Risk Notice

Trading involves risk.

Short-term trading and automated trading are especially sensitive to:

- spread;
- slippage;
- latency;
- broker execution;
- symbol specification;
- margin rules;
- data quality;
- market regime changes.

CFDs and leveraged products can cause rapid losses.

Backtests can overestimate performance because historical data, modeling assumptions and execution conditions may not match live markets.

Past results do not guarantee future results.

Use demo accounts first. Do not enable live trading without independent review, controlled risk limits and forward validation.

This project is educational software and is not financial advice.
